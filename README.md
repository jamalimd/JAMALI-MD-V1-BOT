## 📲 HOW TO CONNECT

### STEP 1

Open the Pair Website.

### STEP 2

Enter your WhatsApp number.

Example

255784XXXXXX

Do NOT use:

+255784XXXXXX

or

0784XXXXXX

---

### STEP 3

Click **Generate Pair Code**.

---

### STEP 4

Open WhatsApp.

Go to

Settings → Linked Devices → Link a Device → Link with Phone Number Instead

---

### STEP 5

Paste the Pair Code.

Your Bot will connect successfully within a few seconds.
